What is GIF?

GIFs are most commonly used to create animation. If you are using GIF for only images, are GIF’s may not be the ideal format to use depending on what you want to achieve or focus on.

GIF is a lossless compression image format, which has reduces image size and maintains image quality. It has features such as interlacing, color palette of 256 colors, and animation.

GIF is the most widely used especially in social media settings. When you go on to image heavy sites such as Tumblr or Buzzfeed, animated GIFs are most prominently seen. To create images that are animated, the simplest and most used method will be the GIF format. Though color and image quality will not be the best, GIF’s ability to create animation more than makes up for it.
