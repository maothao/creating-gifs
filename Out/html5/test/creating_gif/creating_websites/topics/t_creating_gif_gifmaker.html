Creating an animated GIF from images using gifmaker.me

Using gifmaker.me is an easy and free way of converting images into animated GIF format. You can also reformat your images by cropping, resizing, and optimizing. Gifmaker.me provides a multitude of ways to personalize your GIF.

You will need images you want to convert to GIF format.

  1. Go to gifmaker.me.
  2. Upload images to site.
  3. Make any according changes in the control panel.


    You can change animation speed, canvas size, and how many times the GIF can repeat itself.
  4. Select Create GIF Animation.
    After conversion is complete, you will be able to view the GIF, download it, or continue to format your newly created GIF.
