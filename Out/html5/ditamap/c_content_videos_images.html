Creating or finding videos and images

You will need to find or have video files or images before creating an animated GIF. Knowing what you want to use to create an animated GIF will make the process easier.

You can create your own images and videos or take inspiration from television or films. To create you own content, you can take videos or images from your daily life, events, or memorable moments.

Using content of memorable moments on television or film is also another way to create reaction GIFs or GIFs that loop a moment. Depending on the type of method you use to create an animated GIF, you may or may not have to convert videos to your desktop.

  • Creating an animated GIF from video file on Abode Photoshop CC
    Creating an animated GIF from your own personal video files using Adobe Photoshop is easy and simple. Using Adobe Photoshop gives you more control over editing and to easily add text.
  • Adding text to GIF in Adobe Photoshop CC
    Being a bit more familiar with Photoshop gives you the ability to be more creative. You can format and add text to an animated GIF.
  • Turning images to GIF using gifmaker.me
    There are many other methods of creating an animated GIF aside from Photoshop. Using gifmaker.me is an easy and free way of converting images into animated GIF format. You can also reformat your images by cropping, resizing, and optimizing.
  • Creating an animated GIF from video format on imgur.com
    Imgur.com allows you to upload video URLs directly from YouTube without having to download or convert. You are also able to add text to the GIF.
