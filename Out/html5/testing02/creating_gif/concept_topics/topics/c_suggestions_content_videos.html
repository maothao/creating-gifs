Suggestions on creating content and use in social media

Before you start creating an animated GIF, you will need to have a video file or images already picked out. You can find inspiration and ideas from your surroundings and from content online. Knowing what you want to type of content you want to convert into a GIF will also the process of making a GIF simple and help with knowing how to use in social media.



Most often, GIFs are inspired by film and television. It can also be inspired from content created by other users such as vines or YouTube videos. You can also create your own images and videos. To create you own content, you can take videos or images from your daily life, events, or memorable moments. Videos can be taken from cameras, videocameras, phone cameras, and webcams. The ways of finding and creating content are limitless.





Remember that videos and images aren't necessarily created with the intent of turning it into an animated GIF. GIFs are created more as a way to respond to one another through social media, such as through reaction GIFs, which are used to describe an emotion or a specific looped moment. No matter where and how you take inspiration to create GIFs, it provides a fun and interesting way of communicating to one another.
