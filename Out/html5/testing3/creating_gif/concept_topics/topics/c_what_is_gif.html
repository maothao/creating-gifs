What is GIF?

There are a variety of image file formats that can be used. GIF differentiates itself by providing animation, which is how it is most used. Understanding what GIF is can help you to determine if it is the right format for your needs.

GIF is one among many types of image formats, PNG being the most comparable, if not a better option depending on your interests and intended goal. It is an image format that allows users to create a looping animation of a specific moment.

GIF is most widely used especially in social media settings. When you go on to image heavy sites such as Tumblr or Buzzfeed, animated GIFs are most prominently seen. Some of the most famous viral animated images are more than likely to be GIFs. While there are other alternatives to creating animated images, such as JAVA and Flash, GIF is the simplest and easiest method to use due to major browser support.
